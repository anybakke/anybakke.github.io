About me

Feminist, human rights activist, Drama teacher, secondary school teacher, amateur actor, poet, writer, soon-to-be journalist. Studying an MA in Culture and Communication.

Jeg vil gerne undskylde til min stemme, som jeg bad om at være stille,

helt ligesom de plejede at gøre på grund af mit køn. Som om min mening ikke havde værdi, fordi jeg bare var en kvinde. Jeg lover aldrig at tysse på dig igen og heller ikke holde mig selv for munden for at holde dig tyst og lukket, når ord kommer snigende.. Jeg glemte helt hvem vi er i en årrække. Jeg glemte vores power, som de forsøgte at stjæle og knække.

Du fortjener din frihed og du skal høres. Råb højt om din historie, råb højt så dine forfædre bliver stolte. Vi løfter i flok. Jeg undskylder dybt og inderligt til dig min kære, må det aldrig ske igen!

Kærligst din bedste ven - din stemme.

My Story

My inspiration for all my work comes from interactions with others and being a part of different communities. As Hannah Arendt once put it, "To men, the reality of the world is guaranteed by the presence of others"... Like many other artists or writers, I strive to write about the objective truth that I get introduced to in the meeting with other human beings or situations. Some of my poetry has a subjective worldview. When I write articles, film documentaries or write short stories the key point or main aim for me will always be to give the viewer an objective or both-sided view of the story.  Like many other artists or writers, I strive to write stories about the world to convey inequalities and outline societal conditions. I have always been a sensitive soul, and with my reiki education, I have learned how to be intact with my light and power. My poetry collection, “Søvngænger”, is, in reality, a work that I have written and compiled over two years. . Poems that I have written on my journey through life, and where I have had magical experiences that Reiki has given me a new explanation of. The poetry collection is also about reclaiming my voice and working around subjects such as feminism, rape culture, social justice, and civil rights. I am a trained primary school teacher, and educated Reiki 2 healer, attended the Actors Workshop in Barcelona, worked as a drama teacher, and am currently pursuing a master's degree in Culture and Communication. I am interested in the journalistic aspect of cultural studies, societal, and cultural analysis, and creating stories and film content. As a hobby, I perform spoken word on small stages throughout Denmark and abroad. I have been writing my entire life. In the future, I will document my travels through the lens of a camera.
